Pipeway Pond

Solo Project
Tools Used: Mario Maker 2, Figma, Lucidchart, Aseprite
Project Goals: Learn Unity, expand knowledge of C#, improve pixel art skills.
Time span: 1 month

This project is a level I made in Mario Maker 2. The mechanical focus of the level was to teach the player how to use the frog suit in the Super Mario Bros. 3 style, from absolute beginner to advanced tech. I did many iterations with dozens of testers to achieve the final result to ensure that the level was polished.
