PENGUN

Pengun is a project from my C# For Games Designers module, which I later continued to add more polish. I was tasked with creating a top-down 2D shooter, with little other guidance. My approach was to make an arena shooter combining the gameplay of Enter The Gungeon with the upgrade system of Downwell. My work includes the design, mechanics, art, sound effects and programming. The music was made by Ben Reber.
