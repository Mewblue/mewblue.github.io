Rogue Fantasy

This is a personal project I am currently working on. It is a roguelike turn-based RPG inspired by the likes of the earliest NES Final Fantasy games. I worked with sound designer Morgan Smith to produce the sound effects for the game. I managed the priority of each of his tasks to help meet deadlines for his own work and provided feedback so we could achieve the best results possible. I designed the systems and did all of the programming for the game, as well as most of the art assets - this includes the custom font. The assets I did not make are the mushroom, campfire and audio.
